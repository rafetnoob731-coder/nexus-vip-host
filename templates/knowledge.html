Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is the deployment after payment?
Our automated system deploys most hosting plans within 30 seconds of payment confirmation. Dedicated servers may take up to 2 hours for setup and configuration.
Can I upgrade my plan later?
Absolutely! You can upgrade your hosting plan at any time. The price difference will be prorated, and there's no downtime during the upgrade process.
What kind of support do you offer?
We offer 24/7/365 support via live chat, ticket system, Discord, and Telegram. Our average response time is under 2 minutes for live chat and under 30 minutes for tickets.
Do you offer a money-back guarantee?
Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all hosting plans. No questions asked. Domain registrations and dedicated servers have a 7-day refund policy.
Is my data backed up regularly?
Yes, we perform automated daily backups with 7-day retention. You can also create manual backups at any time through your control panel.
